Guidelines:

    THEME:

    "SPIRITUAL BATTLE"


    ENTRY CRITERIA:

    Open to everyone above 18 years of age across India


    ENTRY FEE:

    Free


    SUBMISSION:

    Entries can be submitted through "25thnacsubmissions@chitrahar.ac.in"

    The files must be on or before October 20, 2020. Submissions will not be accepted once the deadline lapses. Entries submitted must be original and unpublished works of the participant and should not exceed 20mb. It should have a title and description. Multiple entries are accepted Entries that include inappropriate and/or offensive content are strictly prohibited and the participant will be disqualified immediately.

  • Visual appeal
  • Extent to which the essence of the theme is adapted
  • Adherence to guidelines
